A fixed-size list of reflected values.

This differs from DynamicList in that the size of the DynamicArray is constant, whereas a DynamicList can have items added and removed.

This isnโt to say that a DynamicArray is immutableโ its items can be mutatedโ just that the number of items cannot change.
